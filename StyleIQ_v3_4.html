How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

Four simple steps from download to your first AI-styled outfit.

1

Photograph Your Clothes

Snap each garment with your phone. AI auto-classifies every piece by type, color, season, and fabric weight.

2

Set Your Style Profile

Answer 5 quick questions about your lifestyle, preferences, and the occasions you dress for most.

3

Get Daily Outfit Suggestions
