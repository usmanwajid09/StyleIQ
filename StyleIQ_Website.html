How It Works

Up and running in minutes.

Four simple steps from download to your first AI-styled outfit.

1

Photograph Your Clothes

Use your phone camera to snap each garment. Our AI does all the categorizing automatically.

2

Set Your Style Profile

Answer 5 quick questions about your style preferences, lifestyle, and the occasions you dress for.

3

Get Daily Outfit Suggestions

Every morning receive 3 AI-curated outfits tailored to the weather and your day's plans.

4

Swipe, Wear, Refine

Accept or reject suggestions. StyleIQ learns your taste and gets smarter with every swipe.
