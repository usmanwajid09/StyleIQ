01 // Company Overview

StyleIQ is a consumer mobile and web application that fuses smart wardrobe management with an AI-powered personal stylist.

Founded in 2026, StyleIQ lets users digitally catalog every garment they own using their phone camera, then uses artificial intelligence to suggest complete, styled outfits every day — adapting to real-time weather forecasts, upcoming seasons, and specific events such as job interviews, weddings, casual outings, or gym sessions.

Vision

To become the world's most trusted AI personal stylist — making confident, weather-smart, occasion-perfect dressing effortless for every person on the planet.

Mission

We empower people to unlock the full potential of their existing wardrobe through intelligent organization, AI-driven outfit curation, and personalized style coaching.

02 // Problem Statement

Despite owning more clothes than any previous generation, modern consumers face a daily paradox: a full wardrobe but "nothing to wear."

17m

Spent daily choosing outfits — 103 hours per year wasted.

20%

The portion of a wardrobe actually worn regularly; 80% sits unused.

92M

Tons of textile waste annually due to over-consumption and lack of visibility.

  • 74% of consumers buy clothing that doesn't match anything they already own.
  • Existing apps are passive photo albums with zero AI intelligence.
  • Weather-appropriate dressing is guesswork; people constantly under- or over-dress.
  • Personal stylists ($150–$500/hr) are inaccessible for most.

03 // Products & Services

Virtual Closet Manager

Computer vision auto-categorizes items by type, color, fabric, and season. Portable digital wardrobe with advanced filters.

AI Outfit Suggester

Real-time local weather integration. Generates 3 daily recommendations considering temperature, rain, and UV.

Occasion Styling

Tailored looks for interviews, weddings, or holidays. Explains the rationale behind every look.

Style Sync AI Coach

Personal style DNA profile. GPT-4o powered chat for open-ended styling advice and weekly reports.

Smart Shopping

Identifies wardrobe gaps — the one piece that unlocks 20+ new combos. Linked directly to retailers.
